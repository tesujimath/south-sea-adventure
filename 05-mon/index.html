Day 3 - Rain

04 July 2021

It rained a lot. I read quite a bit of my book. The rain never really stopped, but when it eased up a little I nipped out on the scooter to buy an umbrella. Curiously there was only one shop on the island able to sell me one, in Avarua right round the other side. There's an obvious lost business opportunity here.
